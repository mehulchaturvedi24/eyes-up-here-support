Eyes Up Here uses Apple’s TrueDepth camera and AR face tracking only to estimate face position, head angle, eye-gaze direction, and whether the user is looking downward during a training session. This processing happens entirely on the user’s device in real time. The developer does not collect, transmit, upload, store, or share face data. The app does not store the camera feed, depth map, face mesh, or any biometric face template. TrueDepth and face-tracking information is used only to provide live training feedback during the session, such as alignment guidance, downward-look detection, and pass/fail session logic. This information is not shared with third parties and is not uploaded to any server. The app stores only local training progress on the device, such as chapter attempts, pass/fail counts, and related session results. This local progress data does not include camera frames, depth data, face geometry, or any face identifier. If you choose to make a purchase, payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive full payment details. If you contact support by email, the information you provide will be used only to respond to your support request. Special note: Face Data and Retention Face-tracking and TrueDepth data is retained only for the live session and is discarded after use. The developer does not maintain any remote database of face data for this app. If you have any privacy questions, contact mehulchaturvedi@icloud.com.
